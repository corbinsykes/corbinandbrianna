Our Story

With the help of The Ohio State University, Corbin and Brianna met on July 21, 2005, Corbin’s 17th birthday, when the two were invited to attend a two-day diversity recruiting college visit. Despite not wanting to attend the event due to a birthday party being thrown in his absence, Corbin reluctantly went and would later realize it was the best decision of his life.

After the visit, Corbin and Brianna did not exchange contact information but Brianna had not forgotten about the fine gentlemen she met that summer come January 2006 when she joined Myspace. Brianna found Corbin’s AIM screenname on his Myspace profile and with the not so subtle push of a friend, ‘Bri8811’ sent a simple “Hey” to ‘koalamonkey5865’ one evening. That very moment sparked a conversation that has never ended... and the rest is history.
